About Us

We've been making healthy food for the last 10 years

Since our modest beginnings in 2013 with a little space in Toronto’s stylish Yorkville locale, Kumar's Kitchen's development has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve solid, Indian-roused takeout food. In contrast to other Indian eateries, Kumar's Kitchen was made with the explicit expectation to appear as something else.
